Note: This app is going to disregard any empty items typed.

Examples:

  1. "," or ",," or ",,," - It doesn't matter how many commas you type, this is going to be considered as empty;
  2. ",item1" or "item1," or ",,item1" or ",item1," or "item1,," - It's going to consider just one item;
  3. ",item1,item2" or "item1,,item2" or "item1,item2," - It's going to consider just two items; and
  4. ",item1,item2,item3" or "item1,,item2,item3" or "item1,item2,,item3" or "item1,item2,item3," - It's going to consider just three items.

If the expected item is empty or contains only spaces, it's going to be disregarded as a valid item and won't be accounted.
